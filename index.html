Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Who is the camp for?

Camp Ezekiel is one camp with two tracks — one for our Youth and one for our Young Adults. Both run on the same dates at the same location, with programming tailored to each group.

How much does it cost and what's included?

The cost is $150 per person for either track, and it includes your food and accommodation for the whole weekend. Payment is made via Zelle to Southregionyouth@gmail.com. Be sure to note "Camp Ezekiel & [your name]" so we can match your payment to your registration.

How do I register?

Complete the official registration form, then send your $150 payment via Zelle. Both steps must be done to secure your spot. Use the Register section above to get started.

Where is the camp located?

The camp is held at Camp Victory, 2953 Willafa Woods Rd, Cartwright, OK 74731. You can open it in Google Maps or check out the venue website.

What should I bring?

Your Bible, notebook & pen, and diary; active wear, comfortable active shoes, and a swimsuit; plus personal items — flashlight, toiletries, blanket & pillow, sunscreen & bug spray, a pool towel, and snacks. See the full What to Bring checklist above.

Who do I contact with questions?

For anything related to camp coordination, call or text +1 (469) 882-6878.
